An anthem for the fading warmth. Written during the last golden weeks of August, when the light starts to change and the air carries a hint of something ending.

This was the first track recorded with the full Jay Trainer Band. There's a warmth to this one that comes from playing with people who know the songs as well as you do — maybe better.
