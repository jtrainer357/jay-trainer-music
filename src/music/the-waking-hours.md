## Liner Notes

Nine tracks from the earliest San Francisco sessions — where it all began. Studio recordings beside raw, unpolished live cuts. The sound of a songwriter awake and testing the edges.

*The Waking Hours* is the foundation. You can hear the ambition outpacing the experience, and that's part of what makes it special. Written and recorded in San Francisco, the album pairs early studio work with live recordings that capture the energy of those first shows, while tracks like *Maybe Off to Wonder* and *Tinseltown* hint at the songwriter who would emerge in later records.

This is where *Tiny Chinese*, *Fractured*, and *Voodoo Child* first appeared in their live forms — recordings that would follow Jay Trainer through every album that came after.
