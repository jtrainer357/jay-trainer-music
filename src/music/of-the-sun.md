## Liner Notes

Ten tracks of folk-rooted songwriting — long, slow-burning pieces that stretch and breathe. Recorded in San Francisco, with songs about witnessing, breathing, and the sun that keeps rising whether you're ready or not.

*Of The Sun* is the most expansive record in the catalogue. Seven-minute epics sit beside three-minute folk songs. The production is warmer, the arrangements more layered, the ambition wider. It sounds like a songwriter who's found his voice and isn't afraid to let it stretch.

This is where *Better To Breathe*, *Fractured*, and *By Cover of a Great Lie* first appeared — songs that would become touchstones for everything that followed.

## Credits
