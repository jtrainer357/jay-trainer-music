## Liner Notes

This record started the way most things in my life start — without a plan, without permission, at an hour when most people are asleep. What became *A Whisper of Ruin* was never meant to be an album. It was a collection of dispatches from a year that broke open and rebuilt itself.

The deluxe edition grew out of necessity. Three more songs appeared after the original release — songs that belonged with the others but arrived late, the way certain truths do. Live recordings from the same San Francisco era captured something the studio versions couldn't: the room, the audience, the moment.

Fifteen tracks shaped by loss, rootedness, and the strange beauty that grows in the cracks. The expanded edition — featuring live cuts, acoustic reworkings, and three new chapters — was written and recorded in San Francisco, with multiple musicians bringing Jay Trainer's songs to life.
