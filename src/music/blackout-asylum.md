## Liner Notes

Five tracks remastered from the original sessions. Stripped to the bone and rebuilt with new clarity. Songs about clearing out, starting over, and what remains when everything else falls away.

*Blackout Asylum* was recorded during a period of deliberate isolation — turning off the noise, stepping back, finding what was underneath. The remastered versions bring new depth to these songs while preserving the raw honesty of the originals.

## Credits

Written and performed by Jay Trainer. Originally recorded 2018. Remastered 2021 in Pittsburgh, PA.
